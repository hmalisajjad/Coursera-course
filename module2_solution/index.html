Our Cars

Toyota

Toyota's long-term plan includes the development of both global and regional automobile models in order to compete globally with a comprehensive line of goods. Watanabe intends to achieve his objectives through a combination of kaizen ("constant improvement") and kakushin ("radical innovation"). One of his future fantasies is a "dream car": a vehicle that cleans the air, reduces accidents, promotes health, is exciting, and can go around the world on a single tank of petrol.
